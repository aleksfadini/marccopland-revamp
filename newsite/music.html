Buying CDs

Please Note:
We encourage you to order directly from USA distributors--Qualiton and North Country; or retailers around the world such as amazon.com, amazon.de, barnesandnoble.com, Saturn, Virgin, etc.; or distributors around the world such as Abeille, musique.com, discplus.ch, jazzcds.co.uk; or directly from the labels themselves. All are easy to find on the web. If necessary, or for CDs that you cannot find, please email your order to marccopland@gmail.com. We will contact you to arrange payment and shipping.
